English

This policy explains how MatchPulse Football Cup 2026 handles user data. The app provides fixtures, scores, standings, match information, recaps, predictions, polls, mini games, notifications, and football content for fans.

Data we process

  • Anonymous account or installation identifiers created by Firebase or similar infrastructure services.
  • Prediction, poll, favorite-team, mini-game, points, leaderboard, and optional display-name data.
  • Push notification tokens if you allow notifications.
  • Device identifiers, advertising data, ad interaction data, approximate technical information, and fraud-prevention signals when ads are enabled.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, performance data, and analytics used to maintain and improve the app.
  • Support messages and email content if you contact us.

Data we do not require

The app does not require your real name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, payment information, or financial data for core features.

How we use data

We use data to operate app features, sync predictions and points, send optional notifications, protect the service, detect abuse, fix crashes, improve performance, measure ads, and provide support.

Advertising and tracking

The app may use Google AdMob and related advertising partners. On iOS, if a use of advertising data is considered tracking under Apple rules, the app will request permission through App Tracking Transparency before performing that tracking. If permission is not granted, ads may still be shown but may be less personalized.

Service providers

We may use Google Firebase, Google AdMob, analytics, diagnostics, hosting, sports-data, and content providers. These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data deletion

You can delete your anonymous account and related app data in the app at Settings → Delete account & data, where available, or contact us at locphd@gmail.com.

Predictions and mini games

Predictions, polls, and mini games are for entertainment only. The app does not support betting, does not accept wagers, and does not allow points to be exchanged for money or items of monetary value.
